Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about my web development services for small businesses and entrepreneurs.

1. How much does a website cost?

The cost depends on what you need. A simple, clean business website usually starts from $150–$500, while more advanced websites with bookings, payments, dashboards, or custom features cost more. I always discuss your goals first so you only pay for what you actually need no unnecessary extras.

2. How long does it take to build a website?

It depends on the complexity of the project, but I’ll provide you with a timeline upfront and keep you updated throughout the process.

3. Do I really need a website for my business?

Yes. A website makes your business look professional, trustworthy, and easy to find online. Most people now check Google before buying anything. Without a website, you lose customers to competitors who already have one.

4. Will my website be mobile-friendly/responsive?

Absolutely. Every website I build is fully responsive, meaning it works smoothly on all phones, tablets, and laptops. Since over 70% of people browse on their phones, mobile responsiveness is a priority from the beginning.

5. What platform will my website be built on?

Depending on what fits your business best. I'll recommend the option that gives you the best speed, security, and long-term flexibility.

6. Will you handle the design, or do I need to bring my own?

You don’t need to bring anything. I handle both UI/UX design and development, so your website looks clean, modern, and easy to use. If you already have a design, I can also build it exactly as you want.

7. Do you provide hosting and domain setup?

Yes. If you need help choosing a domain, hosting, or email service, I’ll guide you through everything. I can also set it up for you so your website is fully ready to go live without stress.

8. Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes. I offer ongoing support, updates, fixes, and improvements. You can choose occasional support or a monthly plan. I’m here to make sure your website stays fast, secure, and up to date.
